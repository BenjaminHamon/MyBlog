About the author

Hi! I'm Benjamin Hamon.

I'm a thirty-two-year-old French guy who worked as a software engineer in the video game industry for about a decade. While it was an enjoyable and comfortable job, I never really felt like it was the path I had actively chosen; I wanted to do something else.

I've loved books and stories forever. I read a bunch of fantasy epics, and I spent my time between books, tv shows, and video games. At school, I was this guy who stayed by himself and carried around a thousand-page book. My favorite books are the Wheel Of Time series.

I've had a hobby of writing since about as long as I've been reading. I wrote short stories, fan fiction, and random chapters for epic novels. However, I never finished or published a book for thirty years, far from it, but Iâ€™ve always loved writing. And then, I decided to get serious about it.

I'm self-publishing my first complete book ever, The Maze Of Your Mind, in October 2023. I hope you'll consider picking it up. I'll be there writing more books, and maybe I'll post a blog entry. Once in a while.

About the blog

This blog features my long streams of thought about fiction and storytelling. I'm not particularly interested in rating media, good or bad; rather I try to write about some aspects I found noteworthy, and I go beyond the initial ideas and impressions. Everything here is subjective opinion and attempt at discussion. Hopefully you'll find enjoyment and meaning here.

The website does not include social media or comments, however feel free to continue the discussion on social media or elsewhere, and maybe poke me so I can have a look. You can reach out to me by mail at blog@benjaminhamon.com. Please also message me if you notice any mistake. Finally, if you like what you read, please share it with people around you. I'm thankful for your time and support.
